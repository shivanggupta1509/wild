About Us

At Wildlife Harmony Foundation, our mission is to bridge the gap between humans and wildlife. In today’s fast-paced world, wild animals often find themselves displaced or infiltrating human territories in search of food and shelter. This leads to conflicts that can harm both humans and animals. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to relocate these animals to safer habitats while educating communities on how to coexist with the wildlife around them.

Mission

Rescue - Relocate - Educate - Advocate

  • Rescue: Aid animals in distress.
  • Relocate: Ensure safe habitat transfer.
  • Educate: Promote coexistence awareness.
  • Advocate: Support sustainable wildlife solutions.
